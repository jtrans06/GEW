About Gayatri Engineering Works


Welcome to Gayatri Engineering Works, a leading engineering firm dedicated to delivering innovative solutions that shape a better world. With a commitment to excellence, cutting-edge technology, and a passion for problem-solving, we are at the forefront of engineering advancements.


"Let's Re-engineer your dreams"



Who We Are


A dream of fabrication took place in the minds of a young Entrepreneur in the year of 1988, in Charkop, Kandivali (West), Mumbai.


We have started business in manufacturing & fabrication of all tupes of sheet metal, lightening and decorative fitting.


We at Gayatri Engineering Works are continuously growing by taking its legasy forward with expanding our business in distribution of building decoratives, interiors, home decor products and complete netting solutions.


we have steadily grown into a dynamic team of highly skilled engineers, designers, and professionals who are passionate about their work. Our diverse expertise covers a wide range of engineering disciplines, allowing us to tackle complex projects with confidence.
